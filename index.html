Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our offline-first solution

How does offline functionality work?
PDFy Studio processes all images directly in your browser. No internet connection is needed after initial download, ensuring complete privacy and reliability even in areas with poor connectivity.
What makes PDFy Studio different from online converters?
Unlike online tools, we never upload your documents to any server. Your sensitive images stay 100% private on your device throughout the conversion process.
Can I process multiple images at once?
Absolutely! Our bulk processing feature lets you convert dozens of images simultaneously, either as individual PDFs or combined into a single document. Perfect for portfolios, reports, or client deliverables.
Is my data secure with PDFy Studio?
Your data never leaves your device. All processing happens locally in your browser with no server interaction, ensuring maximum security and privacy.
Do I need to install software?
PDFy Studio works directly in your browser (PWA) with no installation required. For frequent use, you can install it as a desktop/mobile app for one-click access.
Which platforms does PDFy Studio support?
PDFy Studio works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. It can also be installed as a Progressive Web App (PWA) on both desktop (Windows, macOS, Linux) and mobile (iOS, Android) devices for offline access.
Are there any file size limitations?
There are no artificial file size limitations imposed by PDFy Studio. However, very large images may take longer to process depending on your device's capabilities. For optimal performance, we recommend images under 50MB each.
